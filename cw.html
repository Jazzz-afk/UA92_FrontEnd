Goat Antony at Manchester United

Antony, the Brazilian winger, made a significant impact at Manchester United after joining the club in the summer of 2022 from Ajax. Known for his dazzling dribbling skills, quick feet, and flair, Antony quickly became a fan favorite at Old Trafford. Despite facing challenges in adjusting to the Premier League's physicality, Antony's determination and technical prowess have shown through in key moments.
